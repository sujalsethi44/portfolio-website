Through practical experience and ongoing education, I have developed a strong skill set that enables me to glean important information from real-time data. My proficiency in Python and C++ programming languages allows me to manipulate and preprocess data efficiently, while my grasp of machine learning techniques empowers me to develop predictive models, classify data, and make informed decisions.
